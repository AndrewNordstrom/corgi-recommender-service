    What is Corgi?

    Corgi sits between your Mastodon client and server, enhancing your timeline with personalized recommendations. No code changes needed to your favorite apps.

    Better Content Discovery

    Find posts you'll love without relying on algorithmic feeds. Corgi learns your preferences through natural interactions.

    Privacy-First Design

    Your data never leaves Corgi, and you control what's collected. Choose from three privacy levels to match your comfort.

    Universal Compatibility

    Works with any Mastodon client without modifications. Just change the API URL in your client settings.

    Transparent Proxy

    Corgi seamlessly passes through all Mastodon API requests, enhancing only what you need enhanced.

    Portable Preferences

    Your recommendation profile follows you, even if you change Mastodon instances. Take your experience anywhere.

    Welcome to Corgi¶

    Small body. Big brain. Portable recs that follow you anywhere.

    Corgi is a privacy-aware recommendation engine for the Fediverse that works as a middleware layer between any Mastodon client and server.

    Who is Corgi for?¶

    • Mastodon users who want better content discovery without algorithmic manipulation
    • Developers building Fediverse clients who need a recommendation layer
    • Instance operators looking to enhance their community's experience

    How It Works¶

    Corgi transparently proxies your Mastodon API requests, analyzing your interactions to learn your preferences. When you request your home timeline, Corgi enhances it with personalized recommendations, seamlessly blended with your regular feed.
